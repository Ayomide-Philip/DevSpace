Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how DevSpace collects, uses, and protects personal data from our users.

Data Collection

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number

Data Use

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide services or products
  • To communicate with users
  • To improve website performance
  • To personalize user experience

Data Sharing

We may share personal data with the following third parties:

  • Partners
  • Service providers
  • Law enforcement agencies (EFCC)

Data Protection

We take the following measures to protect personal data:

  • Encryption
  • Firewalls
  • Access controls
  • Data backup and recovery procedures

User Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • Right to access personal data
  • Right to correct or update personal data
  • Right to delete personal data

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We will notify users of changes to this privacy policy Via Email.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at d3vspace@gmail.com

Effective Date

This privacy policy is effective as of 12/2/2025.
